Piano Roll format samples

This page links to sample images from various piano roll formats from most manufacturers, as well as organ and chime rolls. Each sample shows the full width of the roll for about one foot along the length of the roll. Sample images are primarily from piano rolls in the Denis Condon Collection, part of The Player Piano Project at Stanford University Libraries.

Counts of piano rolls in the collection of the Archive of Recorded Sound at Stanford University (as of 24 October 2015: raw data).
