Complete Roll Scans

Here is a list of entire piano-roll scans in full color at 300 dpi resolution. Click on the roll thumbnail to view a reduced 100-dpi JPEG version of the roll (about 10-15 MB in size), or click on the link for the TIFF image for the complete 300-dpi full-color image (large files about 1-2 GB each).

Note that the height of the roll thumbnails indicates the length of the roll (thinner thumbnails are longer rolls). 100-dpi JPEG files are in "visual" orientation, which means that time progresses upwards on most rolls except for Red Welte rolls. In other words, the triangular leader of the roll indicating the start of the roll is placed at the bottom of the image, and bass notes are on the left side of the image. TIFF images are oriented in "notes" orientation, where the leader is at the top of the image, and bass notes are on the left side of the image.

Test Rolls

These scans are various test rolls, either hand-made or produced by piano-roll companies, used to calibrate player-piano playback mechanisms.
